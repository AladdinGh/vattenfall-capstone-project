# Business Case and Project Goal

You are building a Vattenfall-inspired Databricks project.

The company needs a governed data platform that can combine several operational domains:
- energy market prices
- weather observations
- grid events
- reference data

Those domains are not random. They naturally fit a real energy-operations use case:
market prices affect business decisions, weather affects operations, and grid events reveal operational risk and performance.

The weekly capstone goal is to transform these domains into a lakehouse with:
- raw landing files
- bronze Delta tables
- cleaned silver tables
- gold analytical outputs
- final business-facing reporting structures

Day 1 does not finish the whole project.  
Day 1 builds the engineering foundation that makes the rest of the week possible.
